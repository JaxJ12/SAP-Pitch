January 2026
Market milestone
NCAA officially approves jersey patch sponsorships
The NCAA announces jersey patch sponsorships are approved for all Division I programs, effective for the 2026 season. The market opens — early movers gain significant first-mover advantage in their categories.

Q1 — Q2 2026
Market milestone
Early Power 4 programs announce deals — market benchmarks set
LSU, Arkansas, and UNLV are among the first programs to announce jersey patch deals. UNLV signs with Access Biologics at $11M over 5 years. LSU partners with Woodside Energy. Arkansas signs with Tyson Foods. The enterprise technology category remains wide open.
UNLV — $11M / 5yr
LSU — Woodside Energy

Q3 2026
Baylor x SAP
Partnership agreement finalized between Baylor and SAP
Baylor Sports Properties (powered by Playfly) and SAP finalize the multi-year partnership agreement. Deal includes jersey patch rights, technology integration scope, B2B hospitality program, and SAP conference speaking commitments. SAP becomes the first enterprise tech company with a Power 4 jersey patch deal.

Baylor x SAP
SAP software onboarding begins across Baylor athletic operations
SAP technology integration kicks off across Baylor's athletic department — analytics platforms, ticketing systems, facilities management, and athlete performance data. This creates the reference story SAP can use at Sapphire and TechEd: "We run Baylor Athletics."

Baylor x SAP
Partnership announced publicly — co-branded PR campaign launches
Baylor and SAP announce the partnership publicly with a co-branded media campaign across Texas markets. Press coverage positions SAP as the first enterprise tech company on a Power 4 jersey. SAP uses the announcement at Sapphire 2027 to showcase the deal to enterprise customers.

Launch
SAP patch debuts on Baylor jerseys — first enterprise tech brand in Power 4
SAP's logo appears on Baylor jerseys for the first time in the 2027 season opener. 11.7M+ TV viewers see the patch across FOX, ESPN, and ABC broadcasts. McLane Stadium B2B hospitality program launches — SAP hosts I-35 corridor prospects in corporate suites throughout the season.
