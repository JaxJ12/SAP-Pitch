■ Pillar 01 — Brand Awareness & VIK Integration

  • Value-in-Kind (VIK): Baylor Athletics formally adopts SAP Analytics Cloud, replacing legacy backend systems — giving SAP exclusive category ownership and a live higher-ed reference story.
  • Sapphire & TechEd: Baylor becomes a case study presented at SAP's global conferences, extending reach far beyond Central Texas.
  • No Microsoft Conflict: Surface tablets are a Big 12 Conference-level hardware deal. SAP enters as Baylor's school-level enterprise software partner — a completely separate, zero-overlap category.

■ Pillar 02 — Mid-Market Demand Generation

  • I-35 Corridor Lock: Waco–Dallas–Austin is the highest concentration of SAP's $500M–$2B target companies in Texas.
  • Executive Huddle: 12 Baylor Club B2B networking events per year place SAP in front of CFOs, CTOs, and COOs in a premium, captive environment.
  • In-App B2B Funnel: "Learn How SAP Powers This" in-app module converts fans into educated prospects with a direct path to SAP's mid-market sales team.
  • Market Comp: UNLV (G5) secured $2.2M/yr for a patch deal. Baylor (Power 4) commands a premium above that floor.

■ Pillar 03 — Sustainable, Always-On Partnership

  • Multi-Year Compounding: Year 1 proves the model. Year 2 expands the patch. Year 3 delivers the full reference story. Each season adds new executive relationships and case study data.
  • Always-On Content Engine: SAP is featured in every Baylor press release, radio, TV, and digital coverage — 365 days a year, not just game days.
  • Co-Branded Social: Bi-weekly "How SAP Powers Baylor Game Day" content across X, Instagram, and LinkedIn (500K+ combined following).
  • Sustainability Storytelling: SAP's ESG initiatives co-branded through Baylor's green stadium programs — global purpose, local proof.

■ Pillar 04 — Employee Engagement & Talent Pipeline

  • S3E & University Alliance: Baylor's Center for Sports Strategy and Sales becomes an SAP University Alliance partner — connecting students directly to SAP's talent pipeline.
  • Employee Perks: Premium suite access and dedicated SAP employee hospitality packages at McLane Stadium and Foster Pavilion.
  • Hankamer School of Business: Joint case study production with Baylor faculty supporting SAP's thought leadership and recruitment goals.
  • Reference Story Engine: Baylor students co-author Sapphire/TechEd presentations — building a next-generation SAP advocate base.
