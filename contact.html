The Strategic Opportunity

Currently, SAP lacks a major college athletics presence in the American South. Baylor Athletics provides the ultimate entry point into the I-35 Corridor (Dallas, Waco, Austin)—the fastest-growing mid-market business sector in the United States. This partnership directly targets the $500M–$2B enterprise demographic, aggressively shedding the notion that SAP is "only for big business."
