Why Baylor — 4 key reasons
Southern market gap
SAP has zero college athletics presence in the South. Texas is the fastest-growing business market with 200+ companies relocating HQ in 5 years — Oracle, Tesla, SpaceX, Chevron.
I-35 corridor advantage
Waco sits exactly between Dallas and Austin — two of the top 5 US growth metros. The corridor is home to hundreds of $500M–$2B mid-market companies SAP is targeting.
Jersey patch first-mover
NCAA approved jersey patches Aug 2026. SAP would be the first enterprise tech company on a Power 4 jersey. UNLV signed at $11M/5yr. Big 12 valued at $3.5M–$6M/yr.
First real US collegiate partnership
Unlike brand-only plays (Villanova, Stanford), Baylor offers tech integration, B2B hospitality, and a Sapphire/TechEd reference story — the gold standard per Dan Fleetwood.
