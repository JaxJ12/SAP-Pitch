Executive framing

Protect the flagship asset, then prove it works

The jersey patch should close the story, not carry it alone. This section sharpens how Baylor should negotiate the asset, define success, and preserve a premium path around the program's most visible innovation signal.

Negotiation stance

What Baylor should say clearly

  • The annual integration package and the jersey patch are related, but not interchangeable.
  • The patch should be discussed only after SAP understands the broader activation system.
  • The Baylor basketball team can strengthen the story as an additional proof point, not as a reason to discount the asset.
  • If SAP wants the flagship symbol, Baylor should preserve a premium path instead of folding it into baseline inventory.
