Rubric Fit & Measurement

How this activation guarantees memorable impact and verifiable ROI.

01 / Engagement & Behavior

Identify fans through login behavior. We track pregame open rates, repeat session loops, average watch time, and conversion from the live player cam directly into the stats environment.

02 / Education & Brand Lift

Compare favorability among app users vs. non-users via seasonal pulse surveys. We report brand recall explicitly tied to the "SAP Business AI Game Center" rather than generic sponsor awareness.
