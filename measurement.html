The Cross-Channel Lift Multiplier

That matters because SAP is not buying attention alone. SAP is buying a more credible, more repeated, and more contextual presence inside the Baylor ecosystem.

Bulletproof survey methodology

Data integrity is part of the pitch.

To accurately measure mindset shifts and brand lift, Playfly does not rely on flawed methodology. The collection model is designed to survive scrutiny from judges, campus stakeholders, and an enterprise sponsor.

+

No Convenience Samples

We do not rely on convenience sampling from the small percentage of fans who simply walk through the stadium doors on a given day.

  • Avoids over-weighting high-intent superfans.
  • Prevents inflated sponsor favorability from event-day emotion alone.
+

Population Matching

Playfly survey samples are matched to reflect the overall demographic makeup of the total Baylor Athletics fan database rather than a narrow subset of attendees.

  • Keeps the sample aligned to the real Baylor audience.
  • Improves confidence in year-over-year comparisons.
+

Comprehensive Segmentation

We measure across the full ecosystem, surveying a controlled mix of season ticket members, single-game buyers, and out-of-market non-attendees.

  • Captures true SAP brand impact beyond the building.
  • Separates local exposure from broader regional relevance.
