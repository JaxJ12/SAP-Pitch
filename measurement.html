Three Live Use-Cases. Real-time operations in the I-35 Corridor.

By adopting SAP infrastructure, Baylor Athletics turns 300,000+ annual attendees into a massive, live demonstration of SAP's mid-market capabilities. When SAP executives bring prospects to the suites, they aren't just watching football—they are watching SAP software manage a 45,000-seat city.

Module 01

Ticketing & Fan Identity

SAP Customer Data Solutions

Moving beyond anonymous ticket scans. We will integrate SAP Customer Data Cloud to build a unified profile of the fan journey—from ticket purchase to merchandise sales to gate entry, giving Baylor a "Single View of the Fan."

Module 02

Concessions & Supply Chain

SAP S/4HANA

Real-time inventory tracking. If the 300-level concourse is running dangerously low on high-demand items during a 95-degree Texas game day, SAP's predictive analytics flags operations to restock the stand before they run out.

Module 03

Crowd Flow & Operations

SAP Analytics Cloud

Using SAP Analytics Cloud, stadium operations receives a real-time heatmap. If Gate B is backed up by 20 minutes but Gate C is empty, SAP alerts staff to re-route fans and deploy more security, optimizing the gameday experience.
