Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

Who manages the Baylor Athletics sponsorship process? ⌄

All Baylor Athletics sponsorships are managed exclusively by Baylor Sports Properties, powered by Playfly Sports. The primary contact is Luke Holcomb at [email protected].

Are packages customizable? ⌄

Yes — all three tiers are starting points. Baylor Sports Properties works with each partner to build a custom asset mix that fits their specific goals, budget, and timeline.

When would a 2027 jersey patch deal need to be signed? ⌄

To have the SAP logo on Baylor jerseys for the 2027 season opener, the agreement would ideally be finalized by Q3 2026 — allowing time for NCAA patch approval, jersey production, and tech integration planning.

Does SAP need to use Baylor's technology for the Full Partnership or Jersey Patch tier? ⌄

The technology integration is a key part of what makes these tiers uniquely valuable. Dan Fleetwood was explicit that SAP's best partnerships are built around partners who actually use SAP software — it creates the reference story SAP can use at Sapphire and TechEd.

What sports are included in multi-sport coverage? ⌄

Full Partnership and Jersey Patch tiers include coverage across all Baylor Athletics sports — football, men's and women's basketball, baseball, soccer, tennis, and more. Year-round visibility across multiple seasons.

Is there a minimum contract length? ⌄

Baylor Sports Properties typically works on multi-year agreements. The exact term is negotiable — comparable deals like UNLV's have been structured at 5 years. Shorter initial terms may be available for Brand Presence packages.
