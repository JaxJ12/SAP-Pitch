A reimagined premium environment where SAP technology drives the conversation.

The traditional corporate suite is passive. Baylor's premium environment strategy turns McLane Stadium and Foster Pavilion into an active technology showcase designed to accelerate deal cycles with mid-market executives across the I-35 corridor.

01

The Co-Innovation Table

At the center of the suite sits an interactive, SAP-powered smart table. Executives can seamlessly switch from real-time game analytics to reviewing pipeline data and B2B software integrations.

02

Executive Viewing

Every premium seat is equipped with a dedicated SAP interface, offering individualized camera angles, predictive play analytics, and a direct showcase of SAP's edge computing capabilities.

03

Courtside Club & Ribbon Presence

We transition your B2B hosting from the fall into the spring. The premium courtside club offers an intimate environment for closing deals with mid-market executives while SAP branding extends through the ribbon-board ecosystem that keeps the software story visible around the bowl.

The B2B LinkedIn Ecosystem

The SAP Suite is not just a room; it is a content generation hub for enterprise executives. By hosting Dallas and Austin tech leaders in a premium SAP environment, Baylor can trigger organic, high-value LinkedIn engagements. When C-suite guests post about their SAP VIP experience, the brand gains targeted earned media inside the exact executive networks SAP wants to penetrate.

04

End-Wall Command Center

Foster Pavilion is built around two massive video end boards rather than a center-hung scoreboard, which lets SAP own the end-wall command center and ribbon-board storytelling across the arena. That architecture supports the edge-computing narrative on the hardwood while reinforcing SAP's high-stakes performance story in a real basketball environment.
